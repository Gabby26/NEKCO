The orchestra includes a mix of "seasoned" musicians and younger players who sit with their teachers and learn as they play.

Our concert fare consists largely of masterworks from the classical repertoire with some lighter, orchestral-pop selections added.

We present two concerts annually, one in November, and one early in the spring.



Although we do not sell tickets to the concerts, donations are accepted to support the purchase of new music.
